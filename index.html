About Us

Project always helpful aims to provide the skills for the betterment of underprivileged children. We aim to improve their economic conditions with all our might. This project is about engaging, empowering, and inspiring the poor and marginalized communities. This project was started by me, Siddhant Goel, and my friends along with Mrs. Kavita Shinde, president of the NGO - Gali pathshala.

We found education as the best tool to ameliorate their lives. We teach Coding, Public Speaking, Visual Arts and Crafts- painting, sketching, photography, and many more enthralling subjects to these students.

To be an integral part of these bright children click on the donate button to lend a helping hand.
